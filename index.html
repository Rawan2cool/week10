About

Who we are

muve Cinemas is the first home-grown cinema brand and the market leader in terms of screen count in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Established in 2022 with headquarters in Riyadh, it is owned and operated by muve Cinemas Co. muve continues its expansion plan in the upcoming years across the kingdom offering state-of-the-art technology, diverse and immersive experiences combined with unlimited options from our delicious menu to give moviegoers complete and exceptional cinema experience.

  • State-of-the-art technology
  • Diverse and immersive experiences
  • Unlimited options from our delicious menu

muve Cinemas is investing an additional SAR 2bn to open 600 screens in the next five years in Saudi Arabia. These new cinema developments will create some 3,000 direct jobs, providing a boost to the diversification agenda for the Saudi economy.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the different types of movie ratings?

    18TC ,R18 ,R15 ,R12 ,PG12 ,PG ,G.

  • Will cinema staff ask for ID?

    Yes, cinemas staff can ask for photo identification as proof of age. You will be refused entry to the movie if you cannot provide an identification document to verify your age.

  • What is the minimum age to pay for a ticket?

    Children under 3 years old will not be permitted into the cinema. 3 years old and above will be required to pay for entry into the cinema.

  • Do your cinemas provide spaces for wheelchairs?

    Our cinemas have allocated wheelchair seats in the screens. Please purchase a ticket at the ticket counter.

  • Can I bring my children to MUVE SUITES?

    Guests between 5-18 years can enter if accompanied by a guardian however guests under the age of 5 cannot be permitted into MUVE SUITES.

  • Can I see what subtitles are displayed in a movie?

    Yes, the subtitles are listed on the movie information pages.
